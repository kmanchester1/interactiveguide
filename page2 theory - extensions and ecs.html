Extensions and extenuating circumstances (ECs)

During your studies, you might face exceptional circumstances which impact your ability to complete assessments and/or perform at your expected level. This might include, for example, serious illness or distressing personal issues (e.g. bereavement).

It is important that you tell someone at the University as soon as you can about any such issues so you can get the advice and support you need.

As soon as an issue arises, let your module leader and your personal academic tutor (PAT) know. They will then determine how best to support you.
