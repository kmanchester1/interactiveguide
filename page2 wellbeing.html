Personal wellbeing help and advice

Your personal academic tutor (PAT) will be able to offer you help and support for personal circumstances. If you need an extension then please contact your module leader.
As well as contacting your PAT, you may also wish to contact the University Counselling & Mental Health Service (click here to access).
If you have a question or need help with a learner support profile (LSP) then please contact Disability Services (click here to access).
You should make the module leader and your PAT aware that you have a learner support plan (LSP) in place.
