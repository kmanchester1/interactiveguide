Placements help and advice

I have woken up for my shift and I do not feel well enough to attend placement.

You should contact your placement area by telephone and inform them that you are unwell and will not be attending your shift.

If you are going to see your GP ensure you should contact placement again by telephone to inform them how long you are going to be away.

DO NOT email or text your placement, you must contact them by telephone as soon as you know that you will not be attending.
You must complete a placement absense reporting form (click here to access).
Once you feel a little better please complete the absence timesheet and log the missed hours.

Please contact your placement again by telephone to inform them when you will be returning to duty at least 24 hours before your next scheduled shift.
